EduSpark aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. The purpose of SDG 4 is to address the educational needs of people worldwide, providing everyone, regardless of their background, with the opportunity to obtain a quality education. The goal encompasses various targets, including ensuring free primary and secondary education, enhancing vocational and technical skills for employment, and promoting sustainable development through education. Ultimately, SDG 4 seeks to empower individuals and communities, reduce inequalities, and foster sustainable development through comprehensive and inclusive education systems.
